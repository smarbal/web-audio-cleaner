Welcome !

You can use this service to remove the noise of your audio's. Click to upload your audio files or drop them in the zone.
Multiple audio formats are supported such as .mp3 or .wav but you can also drop a whole archive (.zip) if there are a lot of files.
NOTE: Only have the audio files in the root of the archive, no folder.
The audios must ideally be formated like this : `year_month_day_hour_minute_second_*_.wav`. Example: `2023_3_21_10_32_55_ch126.wav`

Once you've clicked on the validation button, your results will be available soon depending on the quantity of data to process !
You will be automatically redirected to the results page or you can come back later and find your results in the history.
You can export the cleaned data as a zip archive containing all files separately or download a wav file where all the audios have been concatenated (in the right order).

Currently the backend uses the MetricGAN+ model with Speechbrain to process and clean the audio files.
